Let $X$ be a compact Hausdorff space.
Let $\mathcat{Vec}(X)$ be the category of complex vector bundles over $X$.
And, let $\mathcat{ProjMod}(C(X))$ be the category of finitely generated projective modules over the $C^*$-algebra $C(X)$.
There is a functor $\Gamma\colon \mathcat{Vec}(X) \to \mathcat{ProjMod}(C(X))$ which sends each complex vector bundle $E \to X$ to the $C(X)$-module $\Gamma(X,E)$ of continuous sections.

The functor $\Gamma$ is an equivalence of categories.
