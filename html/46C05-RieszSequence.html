Riesz sequence


Definition

A sequence of vectors (xn) in a Hilbert space H is called a Riesz sequence if there exist constants 0<c≤C such that

c⁢(∑n|an|2)≤∥∑nan⁢xn∥2≤C⁢(∑n|an|2)

for all sequences of scalars (an)∈l2. A Riesz sequence is called a Riesz basis if span(xn)¯=H.

If H is a finite-dimensional space, then every basis of H is a Riesz basis.
