proof of Bessel inequality


Let

rn=x-∑k=1n⟨x,ek⟩⋅ek.

Then for j=1,…,n,

⟨rn,ej⟩ =⟨x,ej⟩-∑k=1n⟨⟨x,ek⟩⋅ek,ej⟩ (1)
=⟨x,ej⟩-⟨x,ej⟩⁢⟨ej,ej⟩=0 (2)

so e1,…,en,rn is an orthogonal series.

Computing norms, we see that

∥x∥2=∥rn+∑k=1n⟨x,ek⟩⋅ek∥2=∥rn∥2+∑k=1n|⟨x,ek⟩|2≥∑k=1n|⟨x,ek⟩|2.

So the series

∑k=1∞|⟨x,ek⟩|2

converges and is bounded by ∥x∥2, as required.
