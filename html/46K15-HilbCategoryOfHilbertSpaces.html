H⁢i⁢l⁢b category of Hilbert spaces


Definition 0.1.

The category H⁢i⁢l⁢bf of finite-dimensional Hilbert spaces is defined as the category whose objects are all finite-dimensional Hilbert spaces ℋf, and whose morphisms are linear maps between ℋf spaces. The isomorphisms in ℋ⁢i⁢l⁢bf are all isometric isomorphisms.

Furthermore, one also has the following, general definition for any Hilbert space.

Definition 0.2.

The category H⁢i⁢l⁢b of Hilbert spaces is defined as the category whose objects are all Hilbert spaces ℋ, and whose morphisms are linear maps between ℋ spaces. The isomorphisms in ℋ⁢i⁢l⁢b are all isometric isomorphisms.

Remark 0.1.

The category of ℋ⁢i⁢l⁢b Hilbert spaces has direct sums and is a Cartesian category.
