Suppose x=(x1,…,xn) is a point in ℝn, and let ∥x∥p and ∥x∥∞ be the usual p-norm and ∞-norm;

∥x∥p = (|x1|p+⋯+|xn|p)1/p,
∥x∥∞ = max⁡{|x1|,…,|xn|}.

Our claim is that

limp→∞⁡∥x∥p = ∥x∥∞. (1)

which combined yield the result. □
