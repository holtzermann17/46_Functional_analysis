quotients in C*-algebras


Theorem - Let 𝒜 be a C*-algebra (http://planetmath.org/CAlgebra) and ℐ⊆𝒜 a closed (http://planetmath.org/ClosedSet) ideal. Then the involution (http://planetmath.org/InvolutaryRing) in 𝒜 induces a well-defined involution in 𝒜/ℐ and 𝒜/ℐ is a C*-algebra with this involution and the quotient norm.
