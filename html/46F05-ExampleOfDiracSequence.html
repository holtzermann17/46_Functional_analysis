δn⁢(x)=nπ⁢(1+n2⁢x2).

∫-∞∞δn(x)dx=1π⋅∫-∞∞n1+n2⁢x2dx=[y=nxdy=n⋅dx]=1π⋅∫-∞∞11+y2dy=1π⋅arctany|y=-∞∞=1π⋅π=1

for all n∈ℕ+, hence {δn}n∈ℕ+ is a Dirac sequence.

limn→∞⁡∫ℝδn⁢(x)⁢φ⁢(x)⁢𝑑x=φ⁢(0)
