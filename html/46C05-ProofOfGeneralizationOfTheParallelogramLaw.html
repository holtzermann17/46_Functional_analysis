proof of generalization of the parallelogram law


Let g⁢(x,y)=∥x+y∥2-∥x∥2 and m⁢(x,y)=⟨x,y⟩+⟨y,x⟩. Then

g⁢(x,y)=∥y∥2+m⁢(x,y).

Hence, taking x1=x4=x,x2=y,x3=z we have:

∑i=13∥xi+xi+1∥2-∑i=13∥xi∥2 = ∑i=13g⁢(xi,xi+1)
= ∑i=13∥xi∥2+∑i=13m⁢(xi,xi+1)
= ∥∑i=13xi∥2.
