yet another proof of parallelogram law


Define g⁢(ϵ)=⟨x+ϵ⁢y,x+ϵ⁢y⟩, where ϵ is real. Then g⁢(ϵ)=⟨x,x⟩+ϵ⁢(⟨y,x⟩+⟨x,y⟩)+ϵ2⁢⟨y,y⟩. Hence,

∥x+y∥2+∥x-y∥2=g⁢(1)+g⁢(-1)=2⁢⟨x,x⟩+2⁢⟨y,y⟩=2⁢∥x∥2+2⁢∥y∥2.
