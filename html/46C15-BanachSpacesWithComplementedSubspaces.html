Banach spaces with complemented subspaces


Theorem. [Lindenstrauss-Tzafriri]

Let V be a Banach space, such that for each closed subspace M there exists a closed subspace N such that Mâˆ©N=0 and M+N=V (i.e. every closed subspace is complemented). Then V is isomorphic to a Hilbert space (i.e. there exists a Hilbert space structure on V that induces the original topology on V as a Banach space).
