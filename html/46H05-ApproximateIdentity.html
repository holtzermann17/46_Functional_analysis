approximate identity


Let 𝒜 be a Banach algebra.

A left approximate identity for 𝒜 is a net (eλ)λ∈Λ in 𝒜 which :

  1. 1.

    ∥eλ∥<C ∀λ∈Λ, for some constant C.

  2. 2.

    eλ⁢a⟶a, for every a∈𝒜.

Similarly, a right approximate identity for 𝒜 is a net (eλ)λ∈Λ in 𝒜 which :

  1. 1.

    ∥eλ∥<C ∀λ∈Λ, for some constant C.

  2. 2.

    a⁢eλ⟶a, for every a∈𝒜.

An approximate identity for a 𝒜 is a net (eλ)λ∈Λ in 𝒜 which is both a left and right approximate identity.
